# Niuva Product Orientation

## Product Shape

Niuva is one website and one operational platform. Its primary positioning is a
strategic partner for R&D, design engineering, and prototyping; Retail adds a
safe transaction journey without reducing the brand to a commodity marketplace.

## Two Customer Journeys

- **Retail:** standardized catalog discovery, configuration, safe price and ETA,
  checkout, online payment, production tracking, and fulfillment.
- **Business/B2B:** capability and portfolio proof, inquiry/RFQ, quotation,
  approval, design/engineering, milestone, payment terms, and fulfillment.

## Shared Foundations and Separate Lifecycles

The journeys share identity, organization, catalog, materials, inventory,
production, payment infrastructure, shipment, notification, audit, CMS, and
Admin Studio foundations. They retain separate Retail Order and B2B
Quote/Project lifecycles and state machines.

## Current Authority

`docs/decisions/DECISION_REGISTER.md`.

Detailed requirements remain in the approved BRD, PRS, PRD, and Unified

## Deferred Decisions

The Homepage direction is resolved as Unified Homepage with a B2B-primary
narrative and a clear Retail secondary path. Detailed Retail/B2B navigation
treatment remains deferred, together with provider, policy, reservation,
shipping, tax, cancellation/refund/return, protected-scope, production-readiness,
and go-live decisions recorded in the Master Spec and Decision Register.
